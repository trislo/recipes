Ingredients:
 - 1 c coconut milk
 - 1 avocado
 - 1/4 c blueberries, frozen
 - 6-7 strawberries, frozen
 - 1 scoop KS vanilla protien powder
 - 1/2 scoop flax, chia, coconut mix
 - 1 T agave
 - enough  apple juice to get consistency you like

Directions:
 1. Place ingredients, in order, into nutribullet mixer container
    (starting with apple juice to cover the rest)
 2. Give a mild shake
 3. Blend
 4. Add more apple juice as desired
